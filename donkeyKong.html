Volidesus

Space Invader Pac-Man Pong Break-Out Tetris Donkey-Kong

CONTROLS

Left: A, Right: D, Jump: Space
Climb Up: W, Climb Down: S

HOW TO PLAY

Move the paddle horizontally and hit the ball
in a way where the ball will hit the tiles. Where
the tile will dissapear. When you destroy all
the tiles, you will win.
