Volidesus

Space Invader Pac-Man Pong Break-Out Tetris Donkey-Kong

CONTROLS

Left: A, Right: D,
Up: W, Down: S

HOW TO PLAY

Simply score as many points as you can eating the small
dots all around the maze. 10 points per dot (240 of them).
Big points come when you eat 1 of the 4 Big flashing dots called
Energizers worth 50 points located in each corner of the maze.

Each ghosts will chase pac-man in a different way, for example,
blinky will chase the player directly, pinky will chase 2 tiles infront
of the player, clyde will chase the player directly until he is 8 tiles
close, where he will go to the corner, inky will chase 4 block
infront of the player.
