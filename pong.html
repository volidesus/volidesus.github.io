CONTROLS

Player One Up: W, Player One Down: S,
Player Two Up: Arrow Up, Player Two Down: Arrow Down

HOW TO PLAY

Each player controls a paddle, which can move
vertically along one axis, usually up and down. Players
use this movement to intercept and hit the ball.

The objective of Pong is to score points by getting the
ball past your opponent's paddle. When a player fails to
return the ball, the opponent earns a point. The game
continues until one player reaches a 10 scores.
