Volidesus

Space Invader Pong Break-Out Tetris

CONTROLS

Left: Left Arrow, Right: Right Arrow

HOW TO PLAY

Move the paddle horizontally and hit the ball
in a way where the ball will hit the tiles. Where
the tile will dissapear. When you destroy all
the tiles, you will win.

Keep in mind this is an open source project
and can be accesed through github.com
